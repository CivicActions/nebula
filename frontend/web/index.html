About this application

This application was written in 10 days as a submission to the GSA Agile BPA RFQ. As such, it is experimental, pre-production code. Nonetheless, we hope it is useful to medical professionals, and encourage you to try it and give us feedback.

About the adverse event data

Adverse Event Effect data is provided by OpenFDA based on voluntarily reported data, and has various limitations and should not be relied upon for clinical research.

Medical assistance

You must not rely on the information on our website as an alternative to medical advice from your doctor or other professional healthcare provider. If you have any specific questions about any medical matter, you should consult your doctor or other professional healthcare provider. If you think you may be suffering from any medical condition, you should seek immediate medical attention. You should never delay seeking medical advice, disregard medical advice or discontinue medical treatment because of information on our website.

About the prescription data

Prescription frequency data provided by the Department of Health and Human Services. This is a sample prescriptions from approximately 15,000 households. It does not include non-prescription usages of drugs, and has various limitations as explained by the Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality.
